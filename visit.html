Community Science Museum

Visit

Location

The museum is located at:

1 Discovery Way 9020 Lillehammer, Norway

Admission

The entrance is free for all.

There are guided tours of the museum that leave every hour. These tours are 70 NOK per person and include a handy printed guide of the museum.

If you would like to organise a guided tour for your group of 6 or more people, please contact us to arrange the tour.

Accessibility

The museum has wheelchair accessibility ramps. It also has audio guides and braille display signs for the visually impaired.

Upcoming events

Visiting Professor of Aeronautics

Prof Sheila Widnall from the Massachusetts Institute of Technology will be delivering 3 lectures on the development of aeronautics and where the future lies in this exciting 'space'.

These lectures will take place Tuesday 05.01.24 at 11:00, Wednesday 06.01.24 at 12:30, and Thursday 07.01.24 at 12:00. The lecture hall can seat 150 people. First come first serve.

Energetica Exhibition on Loan

On loan from the NEMO Science Museum in Amsterdam, the Energetica exhibition is coming to the Community Science Museum. It will be on loan for the whole month of February 2024. Don't miss it!

Night in the Museum

Get your family together for an exciting night in the museum as you sleep over beside dinosaurs and science displays. Bring your own sleeping bag and get ready to rough it as we go exploring the wonders of science.

The sleepovers are hosted Saturday 13.01.24 to Sunday 14.01.24 and Saturday 20.01.24 to Sunday 21.01.24.

To sign up, please contact us with details of who would like to attend and which weekend is preferred. The deadline for sign up is Sunday 21.01.24.
